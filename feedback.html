Why oceans matter


Oceans sustain life, regulate climate, and protect biodiversity.


  • Produce over 50% of the world's oxygen
  • Support billions of people
  • Regulate global climate
  • Provide food and livelihoods

Threats to Marine Life


Human activities are damaging marine ecosystems worldwide.


  • Plastic pollution
  • Overfishing
  • Climate change

Take Action


Everyone can help protect the ocean with simple daily actions.


  • Reduce plastic use
  • Join beach clean-ups
  • Support sustainable seafood
